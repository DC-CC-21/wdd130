Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide a fun rafting experience to people of all ages. We can't wait to share our unforgettable rafting experience with you!

Audience

Our target audience is families and young adults. The curstomers intrests are rafting and adventure. What will you be doing? You will be rafting. Where will you be? You will be on a river enjoying the beautiful scenery around you. What kind of adventure will it be? Your adventure will be a fun, enjoyable, and unforgettable but very wet one.
